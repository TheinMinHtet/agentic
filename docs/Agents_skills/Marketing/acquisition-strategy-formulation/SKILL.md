## Overview
This skill focuses on how to bring new users into the funnel efficiently. It prioritizes channels based on CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) and LTV (Lifetime Value).

## Usage
Use this to build an acquisition plan that balances low-cost organic growth with scalable paid growth.

## Steps
1.  **Audience Profiling**: Identify where your target personas spend their time.
2.  **Channel Prioritization**: Evaluate potential channels based on reach, intent, and cost.
3.  **Growth Levers**: Define specific tactics (e.g., referral programs, content marketing, PPC, partnerships).
4.  **Experimentation Framework**: Set a process for testing small-budget ideas before scaling.
5.  **CAC/LTV Optimization**: Define benchmarks and iteration cycles.
