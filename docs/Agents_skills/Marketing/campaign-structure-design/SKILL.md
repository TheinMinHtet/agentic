## Overview
This skill ensures that individual campaigns are organized, thematic, and aligned with overall business goals. It prevents disjointed messaging across channels.

## Usage
Use this when you have a specific goal (e.g., product launch) and need to organize the sub-tasks, assets, and channels.

## Steps
1.  **Objective Alignment**: Confirm the campaign goal (e.g., brand awareness vs. direct sales).
2.  **Thematic Architecture**: Define the campaign hook, core messaging, and visual theme.
3.  **Channel Mix**: Select appropriate channels (Email, Paid Social, SEO, Content).
4.  **Asset Inventory**: List required deliverables (copy, banners, landing pages, videos).
5.  **Synchronization**: Ensure messaging consistency across all platforms and timelines.
